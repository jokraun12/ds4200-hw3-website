About Me

Hello! My name is John Kraunelis and I am a fourth year Computer Science and Finance Major at Northeastern University. I am from Braintree, MA and currently reside on Mission Hill, not far from Northeastern's campus. I originally wanted to write about my passion for the financial markets but I decided to go with something a little more extracurricular. Growing up by the ocean I always had a keen interest in fishing, swimming, and all things revolving around the water. I had always loved boats, but never had the chance to own one. However, after a few years working at a restaurant during high school, I transitioned to another job in Boston at Freedom Boat Club. At this job, I was able to work on boats by cleaning them, fixing them, and taking trips to the gas dock to ensure they were always on a full tank!

          Through this job, I was able to get paid for doing something that I was passionate about. I met a lot of really awesome people here, but also learned a lot of valuable technical expertise about boats. After several years of working here, I made the ultimate decision that I would eventually buy a boat for myself one day!
